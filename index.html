Story of Bastrop: Then and Now

The power of narrative in shaping our identities, both individual and collective, simply cannot be ignored. We give meaning to our lives and our truths arise though the stories that we tell. But in todayâ€™s fast-paced world, the craft of storytelling and, more importantly, listening seems to be quickly fading and forgotten ways of knowing. Bastrop: Then & Now represents our attempt at better understanding who we are through the stories that make up our collective history. Some of our stories make us laugh and some make us cry, but they nevertheless all make us. We hope that as you hear and see our shared history that is Bastrop, that you too will take a moment to consider your story as it lives alongside the stories of others. The 2017 Freshmen Class of the Colorado River Collegiate Academy invites you to pause only for a moment and listen to find out more about who we all are as a community.
